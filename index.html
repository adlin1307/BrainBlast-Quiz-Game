How to Play

1

Set up your game. Choose Solo or Multiplayer, pick your favorite categories, choose a difficulty, and decide how many questions you want to play.

2

Answer before the timer runs out. Each question has 30 seconds. Read carefully, think it through, and choose the best answer before the timer ends.

3

Earn points for each correct answer. Every correct answer increases your score. Harder difficulties are worth more points.

4

In multiplayer, take turns. Each player answers the same set of questions. The highest score wins. The losers get roasted — lovingly.

5

Learn something new. Every question ends with a fun fact, so you'll always discover something interesting—even if you get it wrong.
